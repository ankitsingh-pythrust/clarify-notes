Questions, answered

If you are evaluating for your team, start here.

Does Clarify record without consent?

No. You choose when Clarify joins. It respects meeting settings and follows your consent policies.

 Can I edit AI summaries?

Yes. All outputs are fully editable and linked to transcript timestamps for verification.

 What languages are supported?

Transcription supports multiple major languages; detection can be set per meeting.

 Where is data stored?

Data is stored in secure cloud regions with encryption and access controls. Regional options are available on Business.

Need something specific? We are happy to help with pilots and security reviews.
